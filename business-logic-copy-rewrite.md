## COPY PRINCIPLES USED

| Principle | Application |
|---|---|
| **Plain language** | Zero tech jargon. Written at secondary school reading level |
| **AIDA flow** | Attention (hero + problem) → Interest (services + process) → Desire (proof + testimonials) → Action (CTA + form) |
| **Pain-first** | Section 2 names their exact frustrations before selling anything |
| **Specificity** | Results stated as outcomes, not features ("3 new clients", "2 weeks after launch") |
| **Naira pricing** | Grounds the conversation in local reality, pre-qualifies budget |
| **Reassurance** | FAQ answers the fear of being confused or ripped off |
| **One ask** | Every CTA points to one thing: book a free call |
